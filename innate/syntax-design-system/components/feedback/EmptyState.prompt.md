Use when a list, search, or screen has nothing to show. Keep copy calm and specific — a quiet pause, never an apology.

```jsx
<EmptyState
  icon={<Inbox size={24} />}
  title="Nothing here yet"
  description="Saved lessons will appear here as you work through the track."
  action={<Button>Browse the catalog</Button>}
/>
```

Voice: "Nothing here yet." not "Oops! You haven't added anything."
